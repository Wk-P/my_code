一 · 强化学习是什么？— What is Reinforcement Learning?
中 用打游戏来理解强化学习（RL）：
想象 AI 在玩一个游戏：
  • AI 看到当前画面（状态 state）
  • AI 做一个操作（动作 action），比如按某个按键
  • 游戏给 AI 一个分数反馈（奖励 reward）
  • 画面变化，进入新状态，重复
AI 的目标是学会一套策略（policy），使长期总奖励最大化。

RL 与一般程序设计的区别：不需要提前写"遇到情况 A 做操作 B"的规则，AI 自己通过"试错"（trial and error）摸索出最佳策略。
EN Understanding RL through playing games:
Imagine an AI playing a game:
  • AI sees the current screen (state)
  • AI takes an action (e.g., press a key)
  • Game gives a score feedback (reward)
  • Screen changes → new state → repeat
Goal: learn a policy that maximizes total long-term reward.

RL vs. traditional programming: no need to pre-write rules like "in situation A, do action B." The AI discovers the optimal strategy through trial and error.
二 · MDP：把部署问题变成游戏 — MDP Formulation
中 马尔可夫决策过程（MDP） 是 RL 问题的数学框架。在 ECU 部署里：
MDP 概念在本问题中的含义具体内容
状态 st当前时刻的所有信息当前待放服务需求、各 ECU 剩余容量、已累计 AR、冲突标记等
动作 at选哪台 ECU整数 0 到 N-1
奖励 rt这次放置有多好利用率增量 + 违规惩罚 + 终局奖励
回合 episode一次完整的部署放完全部 M 个服务，共 M 步
EN Markov Decision Process (MDP) is the mathematical framework for RL problems. In ECU deployment:
MDP ConceptMeaning hereSpecifics
State stAll current informationCurrent service demand, remaining ECU capacities, cumulative AR, conflict flags, etc.
Action atWhich ECU to chooseInteger 0 to N-1
Reward rtHow good this placement isUtilization gain + violation penalty + terminal bonus
EpisodeOne complete deploymentPlace all M services, M steps total
中 P4 状态向量结构（维度 5N+6+M）： 
[当前服务需求（归一化）        # 1维
 当前累计 AR                  # 1维
 剩余总容量（归一化）           # 1维
 剩余总需求（归一化）           # 1维
 有剩余容量的 ECU 比例          # 1维
 剩余服务比例                  # 1维
 每个 ECU 初始容量占比          # N维
 每个 ECU 剩余容量（归一化）    # N维
 每个 ECU 冲突标记              # N维
 每个 ECU 可放服务比例          # N维
 合法动作掩码（0/1）            # N维
 剩余服务需求列表（降序）        # M维]
服务排序策略：每个 episode 开始时，服务按需求从大到小排序后再放置。直觉：先把最难放（需求最大）的服务处理掉，避免后期无处可放。
EN P4 observation vector structure (dimension 5N+6+M): 
[current service demand (normalised)        # 1 dim
 current cumulative AR                       # 1 dim
 total remaining capacity (normalised)       # 1 dim
 total remaining demand (normalised)         # 1 dim
 fraction of ECUs with enough capacity       # 1 dim
 fraction of services remaining              # 1 dim
 per-ECU initial capacity fraction           # N dim
 per-ECU remaining capacity (normalised)     # N dim
 per-ECU conflict flag                       # N dim
 per-ECU placeable service fraction          # N dim
 valid action mask (0/1)                     # N dim
 remaining service demands (sorted desc)     # M dim]
Service ordering: at the start of each episode, services are sorted by demand in descending order. Intuition: handle the hardest-to-place (largest demand) services first to avoid dead-ends later.
三 · 策略网络（Actor）与价值网络（Critic）— Policy & Value Networks
中 PPO 使用 Actor-Critic 架构：两个神经网络共同工作。

Actor（策略网络 \(\pi_\theta\)）：做决策
输入：当前状态 s
输出：在每台 ECU 上放置当前服务的概率分布 \(\pi_\theta(a|s)\)
结构：两层全连接网络，每层 256 个神经元，激活函数 Tanh（pi=[256, 256]）
类比：老司机看路况（状态），判断"踩油门/刹车/打方向"各有多大概率合适（策略）。
EN PPO uses an Actor-Critic architecture: two neural networks working together.

Actor (policy network \(\pi_\theta\)): makes decisions
Input: current state s
Output: probability distribution \(\pi_\theta(a|s)\) over which ECU to place the current service on
Architecture: two-layer MLP, 256 units each, Tanh activation (pi=[256, 256])
Analogy: an experienced driver assesses road conditions (state) and judges the probability of each maneuver (policy).
中 Critic（价值网络 \(V_\phi\)）：评估局势
输入：当前状态 s
输出：一个数 \(V(s)\)，表示"从状态 s 出发，按当前策略走，预期能拿到多少总奖励"
结构：更宽的价值网络（vf=[512, 512]），比策略网络宽，输出 1 个标量；更大的 VF 容量有助于准确估计长期回报

类比：裁判员不参与比赛，但能给出"这个局面还能拿多少分"的预测。

为什么同时需要两个网络？
Actor 决定怎么走，Critic 告诉 Actor 走得好还是坏。 单靠实际奖励（稀疏且有噪声）很难学习，Critic 提供的"预期价值基准"让 Actor 的更新更稳定、更高效。
EN Critic (value network \(V_\phi\)): evaluates the situation
Input: current state s
Output: a single number \(V(s)\) — "expected total reward from state s under the current policy"
Architecture: wider value network (vf=[512, 512]) vs. policy network (pi=[256, 256]); the larger VF capacity helps accurately estimate long-term returns

Analogy: a referee who doesn't play but can predict "how many more points this team will score from this position."

Why need both networks?
Actor decides what to do; Critic tells Actor whether it's good or bad. Learning from sparse, noisy rewards alone is inefficient. The Critic's baseline stabilizes and accelerates Actor updates.
四 · TD 误差：衡量"比预期好多少" — TD Error
中 时序差分（TD）误差衡量"实际发生的比 Critic 预测的好还是差"：
\[ \delta_t = r_t + \gamma \cdot V(s_{t+1}) - V(s_t) \]
逐项理解：
  • \(r_t\)：第 t 步实际拿到的奖励
  • \(\gamma \cdot V(s_{t+1})\)：下一个状态的折扣价值（未来的奖励按折扣因子打折）
  • \(V(s_t)\)：Critic 对当前状态的价值预测
  • \(\delta_t > 0\)：比预期好（出现了意外收获）
  • \(\delta_t < 0\)：比预期差（情况不如预期）
折扣因子 \(\gamma = 0.99\)：接近1说明非常重视长远奖励。因为 M 步中每一步的放置都影响最终 AR，所以不能只看眼前。
EN Temporal Difference (TD) error measures "how much better or worse than Critic's prediction":
\[ \delta_t = r_t + \gamma \cdot V(s_{t+1}) - V(s_t) \]
Term by term:
  • \(r_t\): actual reward received at step t
  • \(\gamma \cdot V(s_{t+1})\): discounted value of the next state
  • \(V(s_t)\): Critic's prediction for the current state
  • \(\delta_t > 0\): better than expected (positive surprise)
  • \(\delta_t < 0\): worse than expected (negative surprise)
Discount factor \(\gamma = 0.99\): close to 1 means future rewards are nearly as important as immediate ones. Since every one of the M placement steps affects the final AR, we can't be shortsighted.
五 · GAE：综合未来多步的优势估计 — Generalized Advantage Estimation
中 优势（Advantage）：这步动作比"平均水平"好多少？
单步 TD 误差 \(\delta_t\) 只考虑"下一步"，噪声大。
GAE 把未来若干步的 TD 误差加权累加：
\[ \hat{A}_t = \sum_{k=0}^{T-t-1} (\gamma\lambda)^k \cdot \delta_{t+k} \]
其中 \(\lambda = 0.95\) 控制"看多远"的权衡：
\(\lambda\) 值效果问题
\(\lambda = 1\)等效蒙特卡洛（看完整回合）高方差：单次回合结果随机性大
\(\lambda = 0\)只看一步（单步 TD）高偏差：Critic 本身不准确时误差大
\(\lambda = 0.95\)均衡折中实践中效果最好
EN Advantage: how much better is this action compared to the "average"?
Single-step TD error \(\delta_t\) only looks one step ahead — noisy. GAE accumulates a weighted sum of future TD errors:
\[ \hat{A}_t = \sum_{k=0}^{T-t-1} (\gamma\lambda)^k \cdot \delta_{t+k} \]
\(\lambda = 0.95\) controls the "look-ahead" trade-off:
\(\lambda\)EffectDrawback
\(\lambda = 1\)Equivalent to Monte Carlo (full episode)High variance: one episode's result is very random
\(\lambda = 0\)One-step TD onlyHigh bias: errors when Critic is inaccurate
\(\lambda = 0.95\)Balanced trade-offBest in practice
六 · PPO Clip：为什么要限制更新幅度 — PPO Clipping
中 问题：更新太猛会"翻车"
如果一次梯度更新幅度太大，新策略可能与旧策略差异悬殊，导致之前收集的数据失去代表性，训练陷入崩溃。

PPO 的解法：概率比 + Clip
定义概率比（衡量新旧策略的差异）：
\[ r_t(\theta) = \frac{\pi_\theta(a_t|s_t)}{\pi_{\theta_{\text{old}}}(a_t|s_t)} \]
\(r_t = 1\)：新旧策略相同；\(r_t > 1\)：新策略更倾向于选这个动作；\(r_t < 1\)：新策略不太选这个动作。

Clip 损失：
\[ L^{\text{CLIP}}(\theta) = \mathbb{E}_t\!\left[\min\!\left(r_t(\theta)\hat{A}_t,\;\operatorname{clip}(r_t(\theta),\,1-\varepsilon,\,1+\varepsilon)\hat{A}_t\right)\right] \]
\(\varepsilon = 0.2\)：把概率比限制在 [0.8, 1.2] 范围内。若超出就截断，使梯度消失，防止一次更新太激进。
EN Problem: too-large updates cause training collapse
If a gradient update is too large, the new policy becomes very different from the old one. The previously collected data no longer represents the new policy well, leading to training instability.

PPO's fix: probability ratio + clip
Define the probability ratio (how different new vs. old policy is):
\[ r_t(\theta) = \frac{\pi_\theta(a_t|s_t)}{\pi_{\theta_{\text{old}}}(a_t|s_t)} \]
\(r_t = 1\): identical policies; \(r_t > 1\): new policy more likely to pick this action; \(r_t < 1\): new policy less likely.

Clip loss:
\[ L^{\text{CLIP}}(\theta) = \mathbb{E}_t\!\left[\min\!\left(r_t(\theta)\hat{A}_t,\;\operatorname{clip}(r_t(\theta),\,1-\varepsilon,\,1+\varepsilon)\hat{A}_t\right)\right] \]
\(\varepsilon = 0.2\): constrains the ratio to [0.8, 1.2]. If it goes outside this range, the gradient is clipped to zero — preventing overly aggressive updates.
中 Clip 的直觉（用数字理解）：
假设某步动作的优势 \(\hat{A}_t = +2\)（这步很好），旧策略选它的概率是 0.3。
新策略想把概率提高到 0.6（比率 \(r_t = 2.0\)）。
不加 Clip：梯度 = 2.0 × 2 = 4（很大，更新猛）。
加 Clip（\(\varepsilon=0.2\)）：比率截断到 1.2，梯度 = 1.2 × 2 = 2.4（更新稳健）。
这样 AI 不会因为一步看起来特别好就"押宝"，而是渐进地调整策略。
EN Clip intuition (numeric example):
Suppose advantage \(\hat{A}_t = +2\) (this was a great action), old policy probability = 0.3.
New policy wants to raise probability to 0.6 (ratio \(r_t = 2.0\)).
Without clip: gradient = 2.0 × 2 = 4 (very aggressive update).
With clip (\(\varepsilon=0.2\)): ratio capped at 1.2, gradient = 1.2 × 2 = 2.4 (conservative update).
This prevents the AI from overcommitting to one action that looked great once.
七 · 动作掩码：把约束编码进动作空间 — Action Masking
中 核心思想：非法动作永远不会被选到。
在计算策略概率分布之前，先生成一个掩码向量：
\[ \text{mask}[j] = \begin{cases} 1 & \text{若 ECU } j \text{ 剩余容量} \geq \text{当前服务需求 AND 无冲突} \\ 0 & \text{否则（非法）} \end{cases} \]
然后把掩码为 0 的 ECU 的 logit（原始分数）设为 \(-\infty\)，经过 softmax 后概率变为 0。
若所有 ECU 均不合法（mask 全为 0），直接返回全 False 的掩码；在 FFD 降序排列下，此情况极少出现。
EN Core idea: illegal actions can never be chosen.
Before computing the policy's probability distribution, generate a mask vector:
\[ \text{mask}[j] = \begin{cases} 1 & \text{if ECU } j \text{ has enough capacity AND no conflict} \\ 0 & \text{otherwise (illegal)} \end{cases} \]
Set the logit (raw score) of masked-out ECUs to \(-\infty\); after softmax, their probability becomes 0.
If all ECUs are illegal (mask all 0), the mask is returned as-is (all False); in practice this is rare with proper FFD ordering.
中 为什么掩码让学习变简单？
没有掩码时，AI 需要同时学会：① 哪些动作违规（避免选它们）② 在合法动作中哪个最好。
有了掩码，问题①被彻底解决，AI 只需专注于②——在所有合法动作中选最优的。

类比：考试只考你懂的知识点（预先屏蔽了超纲题），学习效率远高于同时还要猜哪些题会出超纲。

与 P5（拉格朗日）的对比： P5 让 AI 可以违规但付出罚款，依赖 AI 自己学会"违规代价太高不划算"。 P4 从根本上禁止违规，约束被硬编码进了环境，AI 永远在合法空间里探索。
EN Why does masking simplify learning?
Without masking, the AI must simultaneously learn: ① which actions are illegal (avoid them) and ② which legal action is best.
With masking, problem ① is solved by construction. The AI only needs to focus on ② — choosing the best among all valid actions.

Analogy: an exam that only tests topics you've studied (off-syllabus questions are pre-filtered) — learning efficiency far exceeds also having to guess which questions are out-of-scope.

vs. P5 (Lagrangian): P5 allows violations but charges a penalty, relying on the AI learning "violations are too costly." P4 prohibits violations by design. The constraint is hard-coded into the environment; the AI always explores within the legal space.
八 · 奖励函数设计 — Reward Function
中 P4 每步奖励（势差奖励 Δar）：
\[ r_t = \underbrace{\Delta AR_t}_{\text{势差奖励}} + \underbrace{r_{\text{terminal}}}_{\text{终局奖励（仅最后一步）}} \] \[ \Delta AR_t = AR_t - AR_{t-1} \]
  • 势差奖励 ΔAR：当前步放置后累计 AR 与上一步 AR 之差。每步 ΔAR 均为非负，且整个回合所有 ΔAR 之和恰好等于最终 AR，形成稳定的 bootstrapping 信号。
  • 无显式违规惩罚：掩码机制已从根本上禁止非法动作，无需额外惩罚项。
回合结束时的终局奖励：
\[ r_{\text{terminal}} = \begin{cases} AR_{\text{最终}} & \text{全回合零违规} \\ 0.0 & \text{有任何违规} \end{cases} \]
终局奖励强烈引导 AI 追求"零违规完成所有部署"。有违规时终局奖励为 0，不再给予部分奖励。
EN P4 per-step reward (potential-based shaping Δar):
\[ r_t = \underbrace{\Delta AR_t}_{\text{potential reward}} + \underbrace{r_{\text{terminal}}}_{\text{terminal bonus (last step only)}} \] \[ \Delta AR_t = AR_t - AR_{t-1} \]
  • Potential reward ΔAR: change in cumulative AR after this placement. Each step's ΔAR is non-negative, and the sum of all ΔARs over an episode equals the final AR — a stable bootstrapping signal.
  • No explicit violation penalty: the masking mechanism already prohibits illegal actions by construction, so no extra penalty term is needed.
Terminal (episode-end) reward:
\[ r_{\text{terminal}} = \begin{cases} AR_{\text{final}} & \text{zero violations in episode} \\ 0.0 & \text{any violation occurred} \end{cases} \]
The terminal reward strongly incentivizes zero-violation completion. With any violation, the terminal bonus is 0.0 — no partial credit.

EN Training loop step by step:
1
Data collection: 40 parallel envs each run 256 steps; AI selects actions with current policy \(\pi_{\theta_{\text{old}}}\) (with masking). Record (s, a, r, s', mask) for each step.
2
Compute advantages: use Critic to estimate TD errors \(\delta_t\), then compute GAE advantages \(\hat{A}_t\).
3
Critic update: minimize \(\mathcal{L}^{\text{Critic}} = \mathbb{E}[(V_\phi(s_t) - V_{\text{target}})^2]\) — make predictions more accurate.
4
Actor update: maximize \(\mathcal{L}^{\text{CLIP}}\) — within the masked legal space, increase probability of good actions.
5
Repeat steps 3–4 for 10 epochs using the same data batch (random mini-batch each time).
6
Update \(\pi_{\theta_{\text{old}}} \leftarrow \pi_\theta\), then go back to step 1.
