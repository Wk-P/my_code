一 · 什么是拉格朗日松弛？— Lagrangian Relaxation
中 类比：罚款代替禁令
假设你是一个停车管理员，规则是"停车场满了就不能再进车"（硬约束）。
但执行硬约束很难（要逐一拦截）。换一种方式：超员进来的车收高额罚款（软约束）。
罚款足够高时，司机就不会主动违规，效果接近硬约束。

数学上：把"满足约束"这个要求从"必须满足"变成"违反了要付代价"。 代价的权重就是拉格朗日乘子 \(\lambda\)（lambda）。
EN Analogy: fines instead of prohibitions
Imagine you're a parking manager. The rule is "no more cars when the lot is full" (hard constraint).
But enforcing it is difficult. Alternative: charge a heavy fine for each extra car (soft constraint).
When the fine is high enough, drivers self-regulate — effect is similar to the hard rule.

Mathematically: transform "must satisfy constraint" into "pay a penalty for violating it." The penalty weight is the Lagrange multiplier \(\lambda\).
中 为什么要用松弛而不是直接掩码？
动作掩码在已知"哪些动作非法"时最好用。
但在某些复杂场景中，非法边界难以精确表达，或者我们想让 AI 在"接近违规"和"不违规"之间灵活权衡， 而不是完全禁止某些动作。拉格朗日方法提供了一种更灵活、也更难调试的路径。
EN Why use relaxation instead of direct masking?
Action masking works best when illegal actions are precisely identifiable.
In some complex settings, the constraint boundary is hard to express exactly, or we want the AI to flexibly trade off "near-violation" vs. "fully compliant" rather than hard-blocking actions. Lagrangian relaxation offers a more flexible — but harder to tune — alternative.
二 · 从硬约束到软惩罚 — From Hard Constraints to Soft Penalties
中 原始约束优化问题（带约束的最大化）：
\[ \max_\pi \;\mathbb{E}[AR] \quad \text{s.t.} \quad \mathbb{E}[v] \leq v^* \]
其中 \(v\) 是每回合的冲突违规次数，\(v^* = 0\)（目标零违规）。
这类"带约束的 RL 问题"叫做约束马尔可夫决策过程（CMDP）。

拉格朗日松弛后的目标（无约束化）：
\[ \max_\pi \;\mathbb{E}\!\left[AR - \lambda \cdot v\right] \]
将约束"违规次数 \(\leq 0\)"转化为目标函数里的惩罚项 \(-\lambda \cdot v\)。 \(\lambda\) 越大，违规的代价越高，AI 越倾向于遵守约束。
EN Original constrained optimization:
\[ \max_\pi \;\mathbb{E}[AR] \quad \text{s.t.} \quad \mathbb{E}[v] \leq v^* \]
where \(v\) is the conflict violation count per episode, \(v^* = 0\) (target: zero violations).
This type of "constrained RL" is called a Constrained MDP (CMDP).

After Lagrangian relaxation (unconstrained):
\[ \max_\pi \;\mathbb{E}\!\left[AR - \lambda \cdot v\right] \]
The constraint "violations ≤ 0" becomes a penalty term \(-\lambda \cdot v\) in the objective. Larger \(\lambda\) → higher violation cost → AI avoids violations more strongly.
三 · λ 的作用：自动调节的罚款系数 — The Role of λ
中 \(\lambda\) 的直觉理解：
  • \(\lambda = 0\)：完全不在乎违规，AI 只优化 AR（可能大量违规）
  • \(\lambda = 5\)（最大值）：每次违规损失巨大，AI 极度保守，可能 AR 也下降
  • 理想状态：\(\lambda\) 刚好让违规代价和 AR 收益达到平衡，AI 自然趋向零违规
关键问题：怎么知道 \(\lambda\) 该设多大？
答：不手动设，让它自动学习——这就是对偶上升法。
EN Intuition for \(\lambda\):
  • \(\lambda = 0\): completely ignores violations; AI optimizes AR only (may violate heavily)
  • \(\lambda = 5\) (maximum): each violation is hugely costly; AI becomes overly conservative, AR may drop
  • Ideal: \(\lambda\) just large enough to balance violation cost with AR benefit → AI naturally approaches zero violations
Key question: how do we know what \(\lambda\) to use?
Answer: don't set it manually — let it learn automatically via dual ascent.
四 · 对偶上升：λ 的更新规则 — Dual Ascent Update
中 对偶上升的核心思想：
如果 AI 当前还在违规（违规率 \(\bar{v} > 0\)），说明 \(\lambda\) 还不够大；就把 \(\lambda\) 调大。
如果 AI 已经零违规，\(\lambda\) 可以保持不变甚至降低。

更新公式（每 20 个 episode 更新一次）：
\[ \lambda \leftarrow \operatorname{clip}\!\left(\lambda + \alpha_\lambda \cdot (\bar{v} - v^*),\; 0,\; \lambda_{\max}\right) \]
符号值含义
\(\bar{v}\)最近20个episode的违规率实际观测到的违规情况
\(v^*\)0.0目标违规率（希望完全不违规）
\(\alpha_\lambda\)0.005λ 更新的步长（每次移动多快）
\(\lambda_{\max}\)5.0λ 的上限（防止惩罚无限增大）
EN Core idea of dual ascent:
If the AI is still violating (\(\bar{v} > 0\)), \(\lambda\) isn't high enough → increase it.
If the AI achieves zero violations, \(\lambda\) can stay or decrease.

Update rule (applied every 20 episodes):
\[ \lambda \leftarrow \operatorname{clip}\!\left(\lambda + \alpha_\lambda \cdot (\bar{v} - v^*),\; 0,\; \lambda_{\max}\right) \]
SymbolValueMeaning
\(\bar{v}\)recent 20-episode violation rateobserved violations
\(v^*\)0.0target violation rate (zero violations)
\(\alpha_\lambda\)0.005step size for λ update
\(\lambda_{\max}\)5.0ceiling for λ (prevents penalty explosion)
中 一次完整 λ 更新的例子：
假设近期 20 个 episode 里有 4 个有冲突违规：
  • 违规率 \(\bar{v} = 4/20 = 0.2\)
  • 目标 \(v^* = 0\)
  • 当前 \(\lambda = 1.5\)
  • 更新：\(\lambda = \operatorname{clip}(1.5 + 0.005 \times (0.2 - 0), 0, 5) = \operatorname{clip}(1.501, 0, 5) = 1.501\)
每次更新幅度很小（步长 0.005），需要累计多次才能让 \(\lambda\) 显著变化。 这让训练更稳定，但也让 \(\lambda\) 的调整速度可能跟不上策略变化。
EN Example of one λ update:
Suppose 4 out of the recent 20 episodes have conflict violations:
  • Violation rate \(\bar{v} = 4/20 = 0.2\)
  • Target \(v^* = 0\)
  • Current \(\lambda = 1.5\)
  • Update: \(\lambda = \operatorname{clip}(1.5 + 0.005 \times (0.2 - 0), 0, 5) = 1.501\)
Each update is tiny (step size 0.005); λ changes only gradually. This stabilizes training but means λ may lag behind rapid policy changes.
五 · P5 奖励函数详解 — P5 Reward Function
中 每步奖励：
\[ r_t = \underbrace{\frac{\mathrm{req}_i}{\mathrm{cap}_j}}_{\text{利用率贡献}} - \underbrace{(\lambda + 0.2) \cdot c_t}_{\text{冲突违规惩罚}} \]
其中 \(c_t = 1\) 若本步放置触发冲突违规，否则 \(c_t = 0\)。
  • 惩罚 = \(\lambda + 0.2\)：\(\lambda\) 是动态调整项，+0.2 是固定的最低惩罚，确保 \(\lambda=0\) 时仍有惩罚。
  • 注意：P5 没有掩码，AI 可以选择违规动作，但要付代价。
终局奖励（回合结束时）：
\[ r_{\text{terminal}} = \begin{cases} +AR_{\text{最终}} & \text{全回合零冲突违规} \\ -AR_{\text{最终}} & \text{有任何冲突违规} \end{cases} \]
终局奖励是强信号：违规会让所有利用率收益变成"负数"，极力引导 AI 避免违规。
EN Per-step reward:
\[ r_t = \underbrace{\frac{\mathrm{req}_i}{\mathrm{cap}_j}}_{\text{utilization gain}} - \underbrace{(\lambda + 0.2) \cdot c_t}_{\text{conflict violation penalty}} \]
where \(c_t = 1\) if placing service i on ECU j causes a conflict violation, else 0.
  • Penalty = \(\lambda + 0.2\): \(\lambda\) is dynamically tuned; +0.2 is a fixed floor ensuring some penalty even when \(\lambda=0\).
  • Note: P5 has no mask — the AI can choose violating actions, but pays the price.
Terminal reward (episode end):
\[ r_{\text{terminal}} = \begin{cases} +AR_{\text{final}} & \text{zero conflict violations in episode} \\ -AR_{\text{final}} & \text{any conflict violation occurred} \end{cases} \]
The terminal reward is a strong signal: any violation flips all utilization gains to negative, powerfully discouraging violations.

中 P5 的 PPO 主循环与 P4 相同（Actor-Critic、GAE、Clip Loss），区别在于：
1. 没有动作掩码（AI 可选任意 ECU）
2. 奖励函数不同（包含动态 \(\lambda\)）
3. 额外的 λ 更新步骤

初始化: λ = 0.1

每轮训练:
    # 1. 用当前策略在 40 个并行环境收集数据（256步/环境）
    # 2. 计算每步奖励（含动态 λ 惩罚）
    # 3. 用 GAE 计算优势，更新 Actor 和 Critic（10 epochs）
    # 4. 每 20 个 episode 执行一次 λ 更新：
    #    a. 计算最近 20 个 episode 的平均违规率 v_bar
    #    b. λ = clip(λ + 0.005 * (v_bar - 0), 0, 5.0)
    # 5. 更新 π_old ← π_new

总训练步数: 5,000,000
EN P5's PPO main loop is identical to P4 (Actor-Critic, GAE, Clip Loss); differences are:
1. No action masking (AI can select any ECU)
2. Different reward function (includes dynamic \(\lambda\))
3. Additional λ update step

Initialize: λ = 0.1

Each training round:

    # 2. Compute per-step rewards (with dynamic λ penalty)
    # 3. Compute GAE advantages, update Actor & Critic (10 epochs)

七 · 为什么在 LT 场景不稳定？— Why Oscillates in LT?
中 振荡循环的成因（LT：15服务/10 ECU，资源紧缺）：
1
资源紧缺时，合法放置空间极小。AI 在探索阶段经常违规。
2
违规触发 λ 上升 → 惩罚加重 → AI 变得过度保守，选择把服务堆在少数几台"安全" ECU 上。
3
过度保守导致部分 ECU 过载（容量违规）或 AR 显著下降。
4
AR 下降但违规减少 → λ 不再上升但也不够降低 → AI 被困在"保守但次优"的策略里。
5
或者：AI 发现保守策略 AR 太低，开始冒险违规 → λ 又上升 → 回到步骤2。

实验结果：P5 在 LT 场景平均每个 seed 有 37.6 次冲突违规放置（5 seeds 均值），远多于 P4 的 0 次。说明软约束在资源高度紧缺时无法稳定收敛到零违规。
EN Oscillation cycle in LT (15 services / 10 ECUs — scarce resources):
1
Scarce resources mean very few valid placements. AI frequently violates during exploration.
2
Violations trigger λ increase → heavier penalty → AI becomes overly conservative, stacking services on a few "safe" ECUs.
3
Over-conservatism causes some ECUs to overflow (capacity violations) or AR to drop significantly.
4
AR drops but violations decrease → λ stops rising but isn't reduced → AI trapped in "conservative but suboptimal" policy.
5
Or: AI finds the conservative strategy's AR too low, starts risking violations again → λ rises → back to step 2.

Experimental result: P5 averages 37.6 conflict violation placements per seed in the LT scenario (5-seed mean) vs. P4's 0. Soft constraints cannot reliably converge to zero violations under high resource scarcity.
中 根本原因对比：
方面P4（掩码）P5（拉格朗日）
约束的执行方式物理上不可能违规违规了付罚款
学习的任务只需在合法动作里选最好还要学"违规代价 vs AR 收益"的权衡
λ 调整速度不需要 λ调整慢（步长 0.005），可能跟不上策略
LT 场景表现零违规，AR=0.67278.9% 场景有违规，AR=0.7006（但不合规）

Constraint enforcementPhysically impossible to violateViolations allowed but penalized

λ adjustment speedNo λ neededSlow (step 0.005), may lag behind policy
